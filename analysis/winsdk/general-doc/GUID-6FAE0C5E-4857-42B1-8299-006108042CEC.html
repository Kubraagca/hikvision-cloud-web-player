File Search

You can search video files stored in device by time, by event (or VCA event), by video tag, or perform VCA search to get the video files as required, and then remotely play back the videos. In addition, searching for pictures or pictures with smart information is also available.

You can also call NET_DVR_STDXMLConfig to transmit /ISAPI/ContentMgmt/search by POST method and set lpInputParam to XML_VideoPic_CMSearchDescription.

The search results are returned in the message XML_VideoPic_CMSearchResult by lpOutputParam.

Note:

Before searching files via the above method, you can call NET_DVR_STDXMLConfig to transmit /ISAPI/ContentMgmt/search/profile by GET method to get the storage search capability (XML_CMSearchProfile) for reference.
